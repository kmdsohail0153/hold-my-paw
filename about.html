Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

We are a group of individuals who aspire to support out nepali dogs out there in the streets of Kathmandu struggling everyday for their daily food, shelter and lives.

Our actual motive is to raise awareness that, being a human, it is our responsibility to protect the voiceless and raise voices for them.

We have been feeding 100+ stray dogs and animals since 2014. And we are continuing our feeding campaign to this day. We spay and neutere stray dogs to keep their population from growing further. We provide rabies vaccination yearly and also provide medical treatemnt and care to sick and injured ones.
